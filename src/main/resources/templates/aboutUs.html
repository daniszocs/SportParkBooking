Sport Park Booking

The largest platform in Cluj for online booking of sports playgrounds.

Are you interested in booking a sports playground?

  • SportParkBooking is a site that helps you make your reservation online for free and easy.
  • SportParkBooking meets the modern needs of mobility and optimization of time spent on recreation and exercise.

    • Do you want to have all the details of existing offers in front of you?

  • All your booking details are now centralized and available online. Thus, the decision no longer has to be made in the absence of important information.
  • SportParkBooking gives you the opportunity to comparatively analyze the facilities and rates of sports fields that correspond to your searches.
  • You can view the general information and time slots available in real time with your friends to make the right decision.

    • How do I make a booking?

  • Through the free online booking system, the customer creates an account, registers and reserves the selected sports playground in the chosen interval with a simple push of a button.
  • The customer receives the confirmation by e-mail. Cancellation of a reservation is also available.
